FAQ

Is this contest free to enter?

Yes!

Do you accept photos taken by phone?

Yes! In fact, we highly encourage phone photography and there will be no disadvantage when we review them with photographs taken by professional cameras.

How many photos can I submit?

For 2025-2026 Spring/Summer Issue, you can submit up to 5 individual photos and up to 10 for group photos which we review and reward them as a unit.

Can I submit photos taken in the past?

Yes

How are the photos judged?

A panel of experienced student photographers will blind judge and vote on entries based on creativity, aesthetic, and relevance to the theme.
