About FRAME! Contest

FRAME! is an inspiration-driven photography initiative that connects student photographers through an online gallery and contest platform. Run by students, FRAME! inherits appreciation for the work with passion and novelty. Take advantage of this opportunity to showcase your artistic talent and compete for prizes, and celebrate the rich diversity and expressive potential of photography!

We value creativity, emotion, and the story behind each photograph. Every photo captures a moment, and we aim to explore the powerful possibilities within that single frame.

Frame! Contest Official Rules

1. Eligibility

All submissions must be original works created by the participant. Plagiarized and AI-generated content works will not be accepted.

Each participant may submit up to 5 works (exact number subject to the official website's guidelines).

2. Submission Requirements

Submissions must align with public moral standards and values. If the work contains sensitive content, a description is recommended upon submission.

Format: JPEG/PNG. Each image should be under 100MB in size.

3. NFT Statement (Non-Tradable)

Selected works will be minted into NFTs by the organizer to record the authorship and creative content.

All NFTs will be sent to a blockchain burn address immediately upon minting to ensure:

• Permanent preservation

• Non-tradability

• No financial value

NFTs serve solely as symbols of artistic recognition and do not involve any commercial activity.

4. Copyright and Usage

Participants retain all ownership of their works.

By submitting, participants authorize the organizer to use their works for appropriate, non-commercial purposes related to the Frame! Initiative.

If a submission involves infringement or inappropriate content, the organizer reserves the right to disqualify the work from the contest or exhibition.

5. Additional Terms

The organizer reserves the right to make the final interpretation and necessary amendments to these rules.

In the event of force majeure causing delays or cancellations, the organizer will issue an announcement with a solution.

For regions where USD is not the official currency, awarded prizes may be converted into gift cards of equivalent value (selected by the winner) or other forms of digital currency.
