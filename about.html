About FRAME! Contest

FRAME! is an inspiration-driven photography initiative that connects student photographers through an online gallery and contest platform. Run by students, FRAME! inherits appreciation for the work with passion and novelty. Take advantage of this opportunity to showcase your artistic talent and compete for prizes, and celebrate the rich diversity and expressive potential of photography!

We value creativity, emotion, and the story behind each photograph. Every photo captures a moment, and we aim to explore the powerful possibilities within that single frame.
